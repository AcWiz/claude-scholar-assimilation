# 结构化 Rebuttal 模板

> 来源：claude-scholar review-response 知识库

## 基本结构

```
# Response to Reviewers

We sincerely thank all reviewers for their valuable feedback and
constructive suggestions. We have carefully addressed all comments
and made substantial revisions. Below, we provide detailed responses.

---

## Response to Reviewer 1

### Major Comments
**Comment 1.1**: [审稿人意见]
**Response**: [回复]
**Changes**: [具体修改和位置]

### Minor Comments
**Comment 1.2**: [审稿人意见]
**Response**: [回复]
**Changes**: [具体修改和位置]

---

## Summary of Major Changes
1. [修改 1] (Reviewers X, Y)
2. [修改 2] (Reviewer Z)
```

## 回复策略模板

### Accept 策略（接受建议）

简单接受：
> We thank the reviewer for this valuable suggestion. We have [修改]. [位置].

接受并扩展：
> We appreciate this insightful comment. We agree that [问题] is important. We have [修改 1] and [修改 2]. These changes are reflected in [位置].

### Defend 策略（礼貌辩护）

礼貌辩护：
> We appreciate the reviewer's concern. However, we respectfully note that [我们的做法] is motivated by [理由]. We have added this clarification to Section [X].

对比说明：
> While [审稿人建议] has advantages in [场景 A], we chose [我们的方法] because [理由]. Our preliminary experiments showed that [结果]. We have added this discussion to [位置].

### Clarify 策略（澄清误解）

指出已有内容：
> We would like to note that we did [已完成工作]. Specifically, [说明]. These results are in [位置]. To make this more prominent, we have [改进].

承认表述不清：
> We apologize for the confusion. What we meant is [澄清]. We have revised [位置] to make this clearer.

### Experiment 策略（补充实验）

已完成实验：
> We have conducted additional experiments on [内容]. The results show that [发现]. These new results have been added to [位置].

承诺实验：
> We are currently conducting [实验] and will include the results in the revised manuscript.

## 特殊场景

### 多个审稿人提出相同意见
> We thank Reviewers [X] and [Y] for raising this important point. We have [修改]. These changes address both reviewers' concerns and are reflected in [位置].

### 审稿人意见相互矛盾
> Reviewer [X] suggests [建议 X], while Reviewer [Y] suggests [建议 Y]. After careful consideration, we have [选择] because [理由].

### 无法完全满足要求
> While we agree that [建议价值], [困难说明]. However, we have [替代方案]. We acknowledge this limitation in [位置] and discuss it as future work.

## 结尾模板

单个审稿人：
> We hope our responses and revisions have adequately addressed all of your concerns.

整体摘要：
> In response to the reviewers' feedback, we have made the following major revisions:
> 1. [修改 1] (Reviewers X, Y)
> 2. [修改 2] (Reviewer Z)
> We believe these revisions have significantly strengthened the manuscript.

## 注意事项

- 每个回复包含 Response 和 Changes 两部分
- 提供具体的章节、页码、表格引用
- 保持礼貌和专业的语气
- 只承诺可行的改进
- 回应所有意见，包括小的 typo
- 不要过于简短（避免 "Done" 或 "Fixed"）
- 避免防御性或攻击性语气
